# Secondary Compute Hardware

## Lenovo ThinkCentre Tiny

### Role
Modern x86 compute platform available for possible pairing with the Toughbook CF-31.

### Intended Use
- Provide a newer CPU platform than the Toughbook
- Preserve full PC software compatibility
- Potential candidate for a dual-computer portable build
- Remain independent enough that the Toughbook can still boot and function on its own

### Design Notes
- Internal mounting may be difficult depending on size, cooling, and power requirements
- Semi-internal or externally attached integration may be more realistic
- Serviceability and portability should be preserved wherever possible
